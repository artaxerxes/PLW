In this exprtiment one set of dots in a circular shape will be displayed.
The direction may change at any time. Please determine its moving direction. 

Press and hold UP key for INWARD moving direction.
Press and hold DOWN key for OUTWARD moving direction.

Press as long as you can decide. If you are not certain, you can press no key. 
But this time should not last too long!

Press any key to continue.
