In this exprtiment two types of colored dots in the shape of a walking man will be displayed. Please PRESS and HOLD the pedal corresponding the number of the color of the dots. If the majority of the dots you saw on the screen is RED, press LEFT pedal. If the majority of the dots you saw on the screen is GREEN, press RIGHT pedal.

Press any key if you understood the instructions above and begin the experiment. Otherwise press ESCAPE to quit!
